Zhu Xue Gao

Description

Pig blood cake is a Taiwanese stack often served with crushed peanuts and cilantro on a stick.

Ingredients

  • Pig's blood
  • Sticky rice
  • Cilatro
  • Peanut flour

Steps

  1. Mix the blood and rice together to form a cake
  2. Roll it in peanut flour and dust with cilantro
