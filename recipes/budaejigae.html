Budaejigae (Army Stew)

Description

Budaejigae, or army stew, is a popular stew in South Korea. It involves lots of meats, ramen noodles, kimchi, and whatever else you can think of.

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Gochujang
  • Instant ramen noodles
  • Hot dog
  • American cheese
  • Kimchi
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Mix the gochujang, chicken broth, and kimchi to create the broth
  2. Add in protein and vegetables
  3. Add in the instant noodles and cheese at the very end
