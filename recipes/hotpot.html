Hot Pot

Description

Hot pot is usually a communal dining experience where people share a bowl of broth and raw ingredients in the middle of the table.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables (spinach, lettuce, watercress, etc.)
  • Meat (beef slices, lamb slices, fish cake, etc.)
  • Tofu (fried tofu, tofu skin, etc.)
  • Staple (noodle, rice)
  • Broth

Steps

  1. Create the broth and wait for it to boil
  2. Add in your vegetables and tofu first
  3. Then add in your other ingredients, and enjoy!
